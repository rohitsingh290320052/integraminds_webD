A gradient is a smooth transition between two or more colors. You can use gradients to create color patterns, add depth to your designs,
and achieve a variety of effects. Gradients are defined by specifying two or more color stops. Color stops are the colors you want to
render smooth transitions among. You can also set a starting point and a direction (or an angle) along with the gradient effect.
